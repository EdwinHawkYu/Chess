## Introduction
Chess is a popular board game played betweened two players where the objective is to trap the opponents King.

Each player has a total of 16 pieces which can be broken up to:
- 8 Pawns
- 2 Rooks
- 2 Bishops
- 2 Knights
- 1 Queen
- 1 King
